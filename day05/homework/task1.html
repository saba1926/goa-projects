Goal oriented academi (GOA)

The Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA) in Tbilisi is dedicated to teaching technologies and programming essential for modern business environments. Students not only gain technical skills but also develop problem-solving abilities and a deep understanding of programming philosophies, including algorithms and artificial intelligence. The academy offers a structured learning path with a one-year foundational course, followed by a more in-depth 2.5-year program tailored to individual interests. Successful graduates often secure positions within GOA
